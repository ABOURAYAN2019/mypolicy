Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Tap the Number app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Consent

By using the Tap the Number app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Game performance data (tap speed, scores, reaction times, game sessions).
  • User preferences (difficulty levels, sound settings, visual themes).
  • Leaderboard data (high scores, fastest times, rankings).
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports to improve performance.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide speed tapping game features and gameplay experience.
  • To save your game progress, high scores, and settings locally on your device.
  • To track performance metrics and maintain leaderboards.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To generate personalized game statistics and reaction time insights.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.

Data Storage

All game data, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share your scores on leaderboards.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, screen refresh rate, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

Cookies and Analytics

Tap the Number does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics to help us improve the gaming experience.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements. Ad providers may use anonymized identifiers to show relevant ads. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

Competitive Features

If you choose to participate in global leaderboards, your scores and username (if provided) may be visible to other players. This is optional and you can play offline without sharing any data.

Your Rights

  • You can access all your game data stored locally on your device.
  • You may request deletion of all data stored locally.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.
  • You can reset your game progress and scores at any time through the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Tap the Number is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or personal information to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will appear directly on this page.
