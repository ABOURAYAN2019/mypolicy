About SUDUKI ZEN

SUDUKI ZEN is an offline Sudoku game designed for relaxation and focus. The game runs entirely on-device and does not require an internet connection.

Information We Collect

  • Gameplay state (e.g., current puzzle, progress) stored locally on your device.
  • Optional preferences (difficulty level, themes, sound) stored locally.
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports (if enabled) to improve performance.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide Sudoku gameplay features and save your local progress.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.
  • No gameplay data or preferences are transmitted to external servers.

Offline Processing

SUDUKI ZEN operates entirely offline. All gameplay, settings, and progress are handled locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or cloud services.

Data Storage

Game progress and settings may be stored locally to allow you to continue puzzles later. You can clear saved data from the app settings or by reinstalling the app.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity or gameplay.

Cookies and Analytics

SUDUKI ZEN does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics only.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to any data stored about you.
  • You may request deletion of data stored locally by the app.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

SUDUKI ZEN is suitable for general audiences but is not specifically intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
