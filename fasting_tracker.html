Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Fasting Tracker app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Consent

By using the Fasting Tracker app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

About Fasting Tracker

Fasting Tracker is an offline intermittent fasting app that helps you track fasting windows locally on your device. The app does not require an internet connection.

Information We Collect

  • No personal data is collected.
  • Fasting schedules and timers are processed locally on your device.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide offline fasting tracking features.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

Offline Processing

The Fasting Tracker app operates entirely offline. All data entry and tracking are handled locally on your device.

Accounts and Authentication

Fasting Tracker does not use accounts, authentication, or any backend services.

Data Storage

Data related to fasting windows may be stored locally on your device to support your tracking. You can clear this data from app settings or by reinstalling the app.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

Cookies and Analytics

Fasting Tracker does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics only.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to any data stored about you.
  • You may request deletion of data stored locally by the app.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Fasting Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
