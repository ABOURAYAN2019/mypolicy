Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Memory Cardz Game app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Consent

By using the Memory Cardz Game app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Game statistics (scores, levels completed, best times, game sessions).
  • User preferences (difficulty settings, sound preferences, theme choices).
  • Achievement and leaderboard data (high scores, completed challenges).
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports to improve performance.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide Memory Card Game features and gameplay experience.
  • To save your game progress, scores, and settings locally on your device.
  • To track achievements and maintain leaderboards.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To generate personalized game statistics and insights.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.

Data Storage

All game data, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share your scores or achievements.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

Cookies and Analytics

Memory Cardz Game does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics to help us improve the gaming experience.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements. Ad providers may use anonymized identifiers to show relevant ads. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

Your Rights

  • You can access all your game data stored locally on your device.
  • You may request deletion of all data stored locally.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.
  • You can reset your game progress at any time through the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Memory Cardz Game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or personal information to play.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will appear directly on this page.
