About TinyQR

TinyQR is an offline QR tool that lets you scan QR codes using your device camera and create QR codes locally on your device. All operations happen on-device without requiring any internet connection.

Information We Collect

  • QR scan results processed locally on your device (not transmitted).
  • QR codes you generate are created locally and remain on your device.
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports (if enabled) to improve performance.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide QR scanning and QR code generation features.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.
  • No scan data or generated QR content is transmitted to external servers.

Offline Processing

TinyQR operates entirely offline. QR code scanning and generation are processed locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or cloud services.

Camera Access

For scanning QR codes, TinyQR may request access to your device camera. Camera access is used solely to detect and decode QR codes and is not used for any other purpose. No video or images are stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

TinyQR does not store scan histories or generated QR content unless you explicitly save them to your device. Temporary processing data is discarded immediately after use.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity or QR content.

Cookies and Analytics

TinyQR does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics only.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to any data stored about you.
  • You may request deletion of data stored locally by the app.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

TinyQR is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
