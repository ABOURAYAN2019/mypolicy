Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the La Boîte à Merveilles – En Poche app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Consent

By using the La Boîte à Merveilles – En Poche app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Study progress data (chapters completed, quiz scores, learning statistics).
  • Gamification data (points, badges, achievements, leaderboard rankings).
  • User preferences (favorite chapters, bookmarks, app settings).
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports to improve performance.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide educational content and gamified learning features for BAC Marocain preparation.
  • To track your learning progress and personalize your study experience.
  • To save your quiz results, achievements, and settings locally on your device.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and educational content quality.
  • To generate personalized statistics and learning insights.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.

Data Storage

All learning data, progress, and achievements are stored locally on your device. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share or sync it.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

Cookies and Analytics

La Boîte à Merveilles – En Poche does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics to help us improve the educational experience.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Educational Content

All educational content provided in the app is for study purposes only. We do not claim ownership of "La Boîte à Merveilles" literary work. The app is designed to help students prepare for their BAC exams.

Your Rights

  • You can access all your learning data stored locally on your device.
  • You may request deletion of all data stored locally.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.
  • You can reset your progress at any time through the app settings.

Children's Privacy

La Boîte à Merveilles – En Poche is designed for students preparing for the BAC Marocain exam. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.
