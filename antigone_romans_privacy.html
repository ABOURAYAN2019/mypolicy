Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Antigone & Romans regional app (an interactive Flutter application mobile created to revise the Moroccan regional French language exam for the 1st year of BAC) handles your information.

Consent

By using Antigone & Romans, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Important: This app works 100% OFFLINE. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data to our own servers.

  • No personal information is collected by the developer.
  • No scores or progress are sent to external developer servers.
  • The app functions entirely on your device for core features.
  • If you contact us via email, we may receive your email and message content solely for support purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do NOT collect any data, there is no information for us to use or share.

  • The revision and interactive features function entirely offline.
  • All progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No user tracking or profiling is performed by the developer.
  • Your usage remains private and anonymous relative to our infrastructure.

Data Storage

No data is stored or collected on our servers. The app works entirely offline for its primary function. Any potential session state (like progress or scores) exists only locally on your device and is not accessible by us.

Log Data

We do not collect any logs, crash reports, or technical information through our own services.

Cookies and Analytics

The app does not use cookies or our own analytics tracking mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

Antigone & Romans is suitable for students and users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app does not require an account or personal information to function.
