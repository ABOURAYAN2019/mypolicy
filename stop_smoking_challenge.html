Consent

By using the Stop Smoking Challenge app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

About Stop Smoking Challenge

Stop Smoking Challenge is an offline app that helps you track your progress, set goals, and stay motivated to quit smoking. The app runs entirely on your device without requiring an internet connection.

Information We Collect

  • No personal data is collected.
  • Progress tracking, goals, and reminders are stored locally on your device.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide offline tracking and challenge features.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

Offline Processing

The Stop Smoking Challenge app operates entirely offline. All data entry and progress tracking are handled locally on your device.

Accounts and Authentication

Stop Smoking Challenge does not use accounts, authentication, or any backend services.

Data Storage

Challenge progress and preferences may be stored locally to support your journey. You can clear this data from app settings or by reinstalling the app.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

Cookies and Analytics

Stop Smoking Challenge does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics only.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to any data stored about you.
  • You may request deletion of data stored locally by the app.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Stop Smoking Challenge is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy
