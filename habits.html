Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the HabitTrackr app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Consent

By using the HabitTrackr app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Habit tracking data (habit names, descriptions, completion history, streaks).
  • User preferences (colors, emojis, categories, notifications settings).
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports to improve performance.
  • If you contact us, we may collect your email and message content.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide HabitTrackr features and habit tracking tools.
  • To save your settings and habit data locally on your device.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To generate personalized statistics and insights.
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.

Data Storage

All habit data is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share or export it.

Log Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as device type, browser version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

Cookies and Analytics

HabitTrackr does not use cookies. Third-party analytics (if used) may collect anonymized usage statistics.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted tools such as Firebase Analytics or Google Crashlytics. These services have their own Privacy Policies and comply with industry security standards.

Your Rights

  • You can access all your habit data stored locally on your device.
  • You may request deletion of all data stored locally.
  • You may opt-out of analytics by contacting us.
  • You can export your habit history at any time.

Children's Privacy

HabitTrackr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will appear directly on this page.
